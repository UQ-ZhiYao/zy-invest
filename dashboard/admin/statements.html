Generate Statements

Generate PDF statements for individual members or fund-wide. Statements are stored in the Documents library and members can download them.

1. Select Statement Type

📊

Monthly Factsheet

Fund-wide portfolio report. Sent to all members. Includes performance, holdings, sector allocation and distribution history.

💳

Subscription Statement

Per investor. Shows all subscriptions with NTA price and unit balances.

💸

Redemption Statement

Per investor. Shows all redemptions with NTA price and units redeemed.

💰

Dividend Payment Statement

Per investor. Shows distribution entitlements with DPS, EPS, payout ratio and dividend amounts received.

📄

Investment Account Statement

Per investor, annual. Full P&L summary, principal transactions, and distribution history for a financial year.
