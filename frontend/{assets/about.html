Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Performance

ZY-Invest was founded in December 2021 as a private investment collective, pooling capital from a trusted network of family and close associates to access institutional-quality investment strategies.

Our Mission

To compound wealth systematically over the long term through disciplined equity investing, transparent reporting, and a fair distribution framework that rewards all participants proportionally to their contribution.

Our Philosophy

We believe that patient capital, combined with rigorous analysis and risk controls, consistently outperforms speculative approaches over full market cycles. Every investment must have a clear thesis, defined risk parameters, and an exit plan.

Distribution Policy

ZY-Invest operates a structured three-tier distribution policy designed to reward investors consistently and fairly.

Interim Dividend

≥ 80% Payout

Based on revenue income after deducting interest and management expenses. Paid at each financial year end (November 30).

Final Dividend

≥ 10% Payout

Based on net profit excluding gross profit. Distributed at end of calendar year (December 31).

Special Dividend

≥ 10% Payout

Based on non-investment income on a discretionary basis. May be declared at any time at management's discretion.
